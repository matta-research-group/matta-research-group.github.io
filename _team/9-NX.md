Neubi Xavier is a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Matta's group at King's College London since June 2026. His current research focuses on the development of AI-assisted high-throughput workflows for the screening of organic mixed conductors for bioelectronic applications. Prior to this position, he was a Research Fellow at the University of Surrey, where he worked on the design of novel electrolyte and anode materials for battery applications using machine-learning potentials, density functional theory (DFT), and high-throughput computational workflows.
