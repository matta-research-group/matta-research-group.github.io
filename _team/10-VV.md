Victor Vladimirescu is an MSci Chemistry student at King's College London working in the Matta research group. His research focuses on molecular simulations of PEGDA based ionogel networks, with an emphasis on building crosslinked polymer systems using SwiftPol, OpenFF, LAMMPS/REACTER, and molecular dynamics workflows.
